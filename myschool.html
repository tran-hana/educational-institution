Hue College of Economics

Background

Hue College of Economics, formerly known as Faculty of Agricultural Economics at Ha Bac Agricultural University, was established in 1969, located in Hue city, Vietnam.
This institution is one of eight members of Hue university and has offered a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs with a focus on economics and business administration.
Its annual enrollment ranges from around 2000 to 2400 students who mainly come from Central Region of Vietnam. It has two main campuses:

  1. Campus 1: 99 Ho Dac Di St., Hue, Vietnam
  2. Campus 2: 100 Phung Hung, Hue, Vietnam

Courses

The college has offered five majors with various courses that are well-designed helps to prepare students for their careers.

  1. Marketing
    • Marketing Research
    • Digital Marketing
    • Principal of Marketing
  2. Accounting
    • Financial Accounting Concepts
    • Taxation
    • Management Accounting
  3. Business Administration
    • Business Law
    • Organizational Behaviour
    • Business Ethics
  4. Finance
    • Financial Analysis
    • Mathematics of Finance
    • Financial Planning
  5. Logistics and Supply Chain Management
    • Transportation and Logistics Processes
    • Transportation Economics>
    • Integrated Logistics

Facilities

To enrich the campus life, the college has offered multiple facilities in both campuses including:

  • Library
  • Meeting Halls
  • Sport Areas
  • Canteen

International Cooperation

The college has cooperated with more than forty international universities to offer several partnership programs as follows:

  • Bachelor degree in Finance - Banking program, partnered with Rennes 1 University, France.
  • Dual Bachelor's degree in Agricultural Economics - Finance, partnered with University of Sydney, Australia.
  • Bachelor's degree in Tourism Business Administration, partnered with University of Hawaii.
